Site Purpose

The purpose of this sight is to inform the local community of the current and upcoming weather forcast. Each town will have it's current weather conditions listed with images to remove any confusion. The images available will be reflecting the current weather conditions. It will be accessible on all device classes: desktops/laptops, tablets and smartphones. Because of this, the site will be responsive and adjust to the screen size. It will be user friendly and designed to be eyecatching and appealing.
